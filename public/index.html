Welcome to Hvuntokrul's Excellent TimeStamp Service!

Pass date (as a unix timestamp or in natural language) as a parameter to the app and it will return an object.

Example use:

https://fcc-time-stamp-ms.herokuapp.com/December%202,%202525

or

https://fcc-time-stamp-ms.herokuapp.com/17543088000000

Example output:

{"unix":17543088000000,"natural":"December 2, 2525"}
